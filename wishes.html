Eid Milad un Nabi

Eid Milad un Nabi, also known as Mawlid al-Nabi, is a celebration of the birth of the Islamic prophet Muhammad. It is observed with prayers, charitable deeds, and festive gatherings. This special day serves as a reminder of the teachings and example of the Prophet Muhammad, and it is celebrated with joy and reverence by Muslims around the world.
